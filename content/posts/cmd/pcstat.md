https://github.com/tobert/pcstat

- Cached 就是 /bin/ls 在缓存中的大小
- Percent 则是缓存的百分比

```bash
# 之前没运行过ls
$ pcstat /bin/ls
+---------+----------------+------------+-----------+---------+
| Name    | Size (bytes)   | Pages      | Cached    | Percent |
|---------+----------------+------------+-----------+---------|
| /bin/ls | 133792         | 33         | 0         | 000.000 |
+---------+----------------+------------+-----------+---------+

# 运行ls之后
$ ls
$ pcstat /bin/ls
+---------+----------------+------------+-----------+---------+
| Name    | Size (bytes)   | Pages      | Cached    | Percent |
|---------+----------------+------------+-----------+---------|
| /bin/ls | 133792         | 33         | 33        | 100.000 |
+---------+----------------+------------+-----------+---------+
```
